Q: How does Aetherum demonstrate human oversight of automated collateral management to NCUA examiners?
AETHERUM-014
The Supervisory Control & Audit Layer provides credit union officers with a documented human-in-the-loop control mechanism. Every supervisory action requires selection of an NCUA-aligned reason category, is attributed to a named officer via JWT authentication, and is attested on-chain. Q: How does Aetherum document AI underwriting decisions for regulatory examination?
AETHERUM-013
Every DACS risk assessment automatically generates a structured underwriting record comprising factor-level breakdowns with weights and plain-English explanations, a risk tier assignment, a comparable peer profile, a plain-language decision rationale, and a regulatory basis citation referencing NCUA 12 CFR Part 723. The record is cryptographically linked to an on-chain compliance receipt and exportable as PDF for direct NCUA submission.

Q: How does Aetherum ensure the integrity of AI-assisted lending decisions for NCUA examination purposes?
AETHERUM-012
Every material lending decision — DACS risk assessment, loan approval/denial, collateral lock/release — generates a SHA-256 hash commitment written to Ethereum via the AetherumComplianceReceipt smart contract. NCUA examiners receive a verification URL for each decision. Recomputing the hash against the on-chain commitment confirms the record has not been altered. Sensitive member data remains off-chain; only the cryptographic commitment is public.
